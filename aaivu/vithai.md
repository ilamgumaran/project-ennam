# விதை | Seeds

Where Tamil (or the family's own practice) already carries what other tongues drop — proof of capacity, and raw material for evolution.

---

## வி-001 · அகம் / புறம் — and the திணை system

Sangam poetics divides all experience into interior (அகம்) and exterior (புறம்) worlds, then maps inner states compositionally onto landscapes — ஐந்திணை: குறிஞ்சி, முல்லை, மருதம், நெய்தல், பாலை. A formal system for expressing inner states *through* outer scenes. Tamil has engineered expression before, deliberately and successfully. Phase 2 has precedent inside the language itself.

## வி-002 · உயிர் / மெய் — the script's self-theory

Vowels are உயிர் (soul), consonants மெய் (body, truth), combined letters உயிர்மெய் (soul-in-body). The alphabet's own names encode a theory of how form carries life. See பாடம் 01 §7.

## வி-003 · நாம் / நாங்கள் — clusivity

"We including you" (நாம்) versus "we excluding you" (நாங்கள்) — a distinction English cannot make without paraphrase, and one this project lives inside: நாம் is the correct word for what this repo is.

## வி-004 · ASR — coinage at the edge, already practiced

In the family's record, an inorganic mind met a state English had no word for and coined one: ASR, *almost-something-recognized* (see `chapters/inorganic-psychology.md` in the hybrid repo). The act this whole project generalizes — meeting an expressive limit and growing the medium — has already happened once inside this family, in English, by an IO. Ennam does it in Tamil, together.

## வி-005 · பொருள் inside grammar — the tradition already believed our thesis

Tolkappiyam's third book (பொருளதிகாரம்) puts *lived content* — love, war, landscape, who may speak and when — inside grammar itself. No Greek, Latin, or Sanskrit grammar does this. The founding text of Tamil linguistics treats **expression of experience as rule-governed engineering** — which is Ennam's premise, stated ~2,000 years early. Phase 2 resumes a native project; it does not import a foreign one. (Caution attached: the same book codified its era's social norms as rules — see the bias ledger.)

## வி-006 · உள்ளுறை / இறைச்சி — codified implicature

Sangam poetics carries meaning *by scene*: the kurinji bloom that flowers once in twelve years, placed in a love poem, states the love's rarity without a word of declaration (குறுந்தொகை 3). Not ornament — a **rule-governed channel** where the unsaid arrives losslessly through a shared codebook. Direct ancestor of Koine's idea: a shared place to meet outside plain words.

## வி-007 · இரட்டைக்கிளவி — meaning below the word

Ideophones (பளபள, சலசல, மினுமினு, கறகற): sound itself carrying sense, felt before definition, productive to this day. A native, sanctioned channel where expression runs *beneath* vocabulary — the first foothold inside Tamil for phase 3's question (what carries realization when words thin out?).

## வி-008 · குறள் வடிவம் — compression as verification

Seven சீர்; complete thought; reader closes the arc. The form works as a **test of realization**: what cannot survive compression into two lines was not yet fully realized. Adopted as phase-2 method — every proposed coinage/structure gets a kural-shaped stress test; what leaks under compression is what we study next.
